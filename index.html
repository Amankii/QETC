WELCOME TO QETC!

QETC Education & Training Consultancy SDN. BHD was founded by a group of professionals who are highly skilled in education and technology. We offer advanced IT solutions to education institutions around the world mainly in east African countries. It was commenced to bring the rapid technological advances from advancing countries such as Malaysia to east African countries where the education is conducted and managed mainly on manual. With these needs in mind, QETC set out to provide optimum and customized educational technology solutions.In QETC we believe ICT helps to build an efficient educational institution where teaching, learning and research can be managed and measured through systems to ensure maximum efficiency in all aspects. QETC is focusing exclusively in high quality and cost-effective systems development and implementation
